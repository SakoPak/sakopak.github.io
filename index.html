I'm Sako Pak

Software Developer with a Passion for Accessibility

Find Out More

I'm a full stack engineer and photographer based in Seattle.

I was inspired to transition to software engineering after my husband lost his vision due to a degenerative disease.

Through watching him use screen readers to study and read, voice and face recognition to make simple purchases, I witnessed how technology creates accessible products that drastically improve the quality of life for all people, especially those with disabilities.

I enjoy making tech beautiful, accessible and easy to use.
